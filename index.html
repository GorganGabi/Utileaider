Services

Consulting

Utileaider offers consulting solutions for sustainable smart street lighting. It helps to reduce carbon footprints and establish sustainability for lighting solutions across industry and utility.

Utileaider offers consulting solutions for smart grid. It helps industry and utilities to build deeper understanding of meter communication infrastructure to setup their smart grid road map.

Training

Utileaider training program target power distribution personnel directly or indirectly related to smart grid implementation. The training program addresses skill shortage in smart grid domain.

Our training includes

  • Advance Metering Infrastructure
  • Demand Side Management
  • Renewable integration to grid
